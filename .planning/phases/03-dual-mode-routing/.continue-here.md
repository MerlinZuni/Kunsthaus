## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)

| Pattern | Description | Severity | Prevention Mechanism |
|---------|-------------|----------|---------------------|
| Pre-emitting stub contracts | Wiring a silent-stub control (e.g. the headphones button) to dispatch a custom event "so 03-04 can just listen" creates a dead contract that future plans must honor or rip out. | advisory | Silent stubs stay silent — no event, no handler, no `window.__foo` hook — until the consuming plan exists. |
| `/videos/` vs `/video/` asset path drift | Research file used `/videos/` but on-disk directory is `/video/`. Following research blindly would have 404'd the hero. | advisory | Verify asset paths against `ls public/` before committing markup that references them. |
| `transition:persist` on autoplay video | Adding `transition:persist` to an autoplay HTML5 `<video>` causes unreliable iOS autoplay across View Transitions. | advisory | Keep background videos plain HTML5; do not mix with Astro's persist directive. |
